NOT All BATTLES are worth winning

I had been an introvert lately, and talkng to others felt to be an great deal for me. For as much as i would love to connect with others to hear about the gems, the stories, the experiences they have mined out of life lately, i simply couldn't. There are few key things to which i have pondered to like sharing ideas and ones own experiences and learning about their experiences isn't always easy, especially when we don't agree with each other thoughts. Whenever i met them whether it be a club gathering, social-get-together with friends or family i always had some disagreement and that was quite inevitable. But just because they're missing something or making a logical leap it doesn't mean that we have to fight to prove a point.
Here is a small story from my life experiences and how i dealt with it.


CORONA ka ilaz from Whatsapp University

So this was a time when pandemic had started in our country. Myself being a student in a college had to stay in another city. One day my mom called over the phone, being worried about my health, she spoke to me about some Home Remedies to cure corona at home. And when i asked her from where did she learn about this remedy, to my surprise she concluded that was a whatsapp foreward message, and she believed in it. Now we being the new generation we know it's a fake news. I am sure some of you might be able to relate with me. Now here i could have had told her several facts and made her realise that she was wrong. But on the contrary i said ok mom i will definitely try this and after a while we both disconnected our call. And next day I called her and told her that "Ma yeh wala nuska maine try kiya aur yeh kaam nhi krta" And i explained her with my theory that Ma this is a type of mask that we would need against fighting corona.

Now what's the point of this story

There are certain arguments, battles, that are not worth winning. So rather being arrogant about this that i know better, we must support them, we must make it as a collaboration that yes we are in this together. We are a team. Because they have lived life from their perspective which is different form us. So from this we come to the our topic that there is no need to ruin our relationships on small topics. There are certain times in our day-to-day life where we could stand to prove a point. There are some times when we are fighting our battles, winning our battles all together and in that moment we forget that to take a decision that which battles should we really fight and which we should and i guess this is what it comes from EXPERIENCE. And thus i conclude this by saying that Not all Battles are worth our Time
